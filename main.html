Faroe Islands

A Place at the Edge of the World

Learn more

Faroe Islands are located in the North Atlantic, in the open ocean

Officially, they belong to Denmark, but in reality, life on the Faroe Islands is governed more by their own laws and rules than by the Danish crown.

There are a total of 18 islands in the Faroes, and all but the last one, Little Dimun, are inhabited
